# User Stories

## Super Admins

1. cannot delete their account.
1. can update their profile information.
1. can update their profile photo.
1. can add other super admins.
1. can CRUD admins.
1. can CRUD users.
1. can CRUD delivery regions.
1. can CRUD delivery areas.
1. can CRUD product categories.
1. can CRUD products.
1. can CRUD product offers.
1. can RUD sales.
1. can RUD contact messages.
1. can CRUD blog categories.
1. can CRUD blogs.

## Admins

1. cannot delete their account.
1. can update their profile information.
1. can update their profile photo.
1. can CRUD admins.
1. can CRUD users.
1. can CRUD delivery regions.
1. can CRUD delivery areas.
1. can CRUD product categories.
1. can CRUD products.
1. can CRUD product offers.
1. can RUD sales.
1. can RUD contact messages.
1. can CRUD blog categories.
1. can CRUD blogs.

## Cashier

1. cannot delete their account.
1. can update their profile information.
1. can update their profile photo.
1. can RU sales delivery and processing statuses.

## Users

1. can delete their account.
1. can view shop page.
1. can search for products in the shop page.
1. can view ratings for products.
1. can rate products when logged in.
1. can view product details page.
1. can view products categorized page.
1. can add items to cart without logging in.
1. can view cart items without loggin in.
1. can checkout once they login.
1. can view their purchases with delivery and payment statuses.
1. can read and add comments to blogs.
1. can send contact form messages.
